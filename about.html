ABOUT

The Honey Hive Gallery is meant to be an inviting and accessible creative space for all kinds of people in the SF/Bay Area art community and beyond. The gallery is mostly known as one of the very few places in San Francisco to host art and music events open to all ages! The vast majority of events here are and have been donation-based(meaning N.O.T.A.F.L.O.F.). The gallery also houses a small group of local artists collectively renting personal studio spaces. Past events have included a wide variety of showcases and workshops, such as: art shows, concerts, writer's workshops, poetry slams, open mics, figure drawing, screen printing classes, yoga classes, etc.
